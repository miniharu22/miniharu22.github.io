- **Device Performance Category & Item**  
  - **On-current(Resistance)** 
    - Invertion Layer Thickness ($$T_{inv}$$)
    - Velocity (mobility)  
    - Contact Resistance  
    - S/D Resistance  
  - **Leakage**  
    - DIBL(Drain Induced Barrier Lowering)  
    - Sub-$$V_t$$ Swing  
  - **Parasitic Capacitance**  
    - Low-k Spacer  
    - ULK(Ultra Low-k) IMD(Inter Metal Dielectric)  

&nbsp;

- Device의 Performance 향상을 위해서는 다음이 필요  
  - On-current 향상 : Mobility ↑ & $$T_{inv}$$ ↓  
  - Short Channel Effects ↓  
  - Parasitic R & C ↓
